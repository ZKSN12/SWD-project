Our Stories

Who We Are

LovingHeart is no-kill animal shelter in Greenwood area, Indiana. Since 2005, LovingHeart has placed over 3,000 dogs and cats in permanent homes. We receive no government support, relying solely on donations of time, service, and money from our supporters, the public, and area businesses.

What We Do

LovingHeart promotes public awareness regarding the value of pets, animal welfare, pet overpopulation, and quality human-animal companionship through education and outreach programs.
